AI App Builder — N20 Studio Taste Plan

Locked visual-system direction and delivery sequence for a dark-first studio refinement.

Decisions locked 2026-07-24 after two questionnaire rounds.

Decisions

  • Direction: precision instrument with one warm degree: Linear/Figma density plus tasteful interactions and guiding UI.
  • Type: Geist Sans and Geist Mono through next/font; one meta-type token scale and tabular numerals.
  • Density and depth: 28px compact IDE rows against an airy canvas; recessed surface-0 canvas, surface-1 panels, surface-2 overlays/composer, tier-tinted hairlines, and shadows only on overlays.
  • Color: muted olive-green interactive, selection, and brand accent; teal success, amber warning, red error, and subtly warm neutrals.
  • Motion: micro-motion only—existing tokens plus springs for collapse/popovers and entrance fades. Choreography is later.
  • Theme: full dark/light parity, designed dark-first.
  • Panels: left panel becomes stacked, resizable, collapsible Pages/Layers/Palette sections; right panel keeps tabs, with stacked resizable collapsible sections inside each tab.
  • Guidance: rich empty states with next-step CTAs, hover reveals, first-use hints, and consistent focus/keyboard treatment—no tours.

Work items

  1. S — Token foundation: olive accent, teal/amber/red semantics, warm neutrals, surface tiers, meta-type tokens, 6/8/10 radius rule, and Geist pair. This is the multiplier PR.
  2. M — Builder shell: header identity and weight, tiered panel chrome, recessed canvas well plus artboard elevation, tab strips, collapse rails, empty/loading states, and agent-status color correction.
  3. M — Left-panel sections: resizable section heights, per-section collapse, and VS Code-style header rows with inline actions.
  4. M — Right-panel structure: tab contents organized by a shared stacked-section primitive, coordinated with N22.
  5. S/M — Layers and palette: human display name primary, mono ID demoted to hover/meta, olive selection, tree guides, hover actions, palette grouping, and search.
  6. S — Chat and dashboard polish: surface-2 composer, run rail, and app-card identity tiles on the new tokens.
  7. S — Affordance sweep: empty-state CTAs, hover reveals, and focus treatment.

Sequence: 1 → 2 → 3/4 in parallel → 5/6/7. The shared Section primitive coordinates with N22.
